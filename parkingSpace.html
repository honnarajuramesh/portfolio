Parking Space management Android App

Introducing the 'Parking Space Management' Android Application, a cutting-edge solution designed to simplify parking space management for owners and enhance the parking experience for users. Developed by me, this app brings efficiency and convenience to the world of parking.

Tech stack

Here is the tech stack used in this project:

  • React Native
  • MongoDB
  • netlify

Key Features:

An Android app designed and developed by me using React Native. This app simplifies the process of managing parking spaces efficiently and effectively.

  • Random Space Selection: For those who prefer a bit of spontaneity, our app offers a unique 'Random Space' selection feature, making parking exciting and unpredictable..
  • Availability: Users can check the real-time availability of parking spaces, ensuring they can secure a spot before arrival.
  • Calculation: The application allows space owners to calculate parking fees based on time, providing a transparent and fair pricing system.
